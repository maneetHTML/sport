1. Baseball – 500 Million Fans

    2. Not only is baseball a pastime sport in America, but it is also most popular in the Caribbean and East Asia. The Major League Baseball (MLB), the pinnacle of baseball leagues globally, has about 500 million fans globally. In a test of gender equality, male players grossly dominate the sport. Baseball attracts sponsorships by top brands such as Mastercard, Chevrolet, and Papa John’s.
